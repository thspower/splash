Infernal Tavern

A Haven for Intrepid Souls

Environment made for Rockers

Welcome to Infernal Tavern, the ultimate haven for those who live and breathe the spirit of rock! Amidst the glow of neons and the sound of screeching guitars, our tavern offers a unique experience that celebrates the rebellion and passion of rockers of all generations.

What will you find

  • Exclusive selection of themed drinks
  • Live shows with emerging and established bands
  • Themed events and air guitar competitions
  • Environment decorated with iconic album covers
  • Meeting point for the rockers community
  • Space to discover new musical talents

At Infernal Tavern, every night is a new adventure full of music, energy and camaraderie. Come be part of this vibrant community and discover why our tavern is the perfect place for all rock lovers.

Events and Activities

  • Covers Night: Local and emerging bands feature rock classics.
  • Karaoke Rock:Get on stage and sing your songs rock’n’roll favorites.
  • Music Workshops: Learn to play instruments or write songs with experienced musicians.
  • Vinyl Sessions: Discover and listen to albums classics in our vinyl collection.
  • Theme Nights: Celebrations dedicated to subgenres of rock, from punk to metal.
  • Art exhibitions: Works of art inspired by rock created by local artists.
